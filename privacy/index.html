Privacy

What we collect and why

Short version: we keep only what we need.

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Google sign-in supplies your name, email address, profile image URL, email verification status, and a stable Google account identifier.

How we use it

We store your email address in Makeable's waitlist storage so we can manage the waitlist and send pilot-access communications. The current hosted deployment uses Netlify Blobs. If we connect another waitlist service, the record may be securely delivered to that service for the same purpose. If you join through Google sign-in, we may also keep your name and profile image to make the experience friendlier in the product.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your email address or Google profile data.
  • We do not use the Google sign-in data for ad targeting.
  • We do not ask for more Google data than we need for the waitlist.

Retention and removal

We keep waitlist records until they are no longer needed for pilot access or product communication. You can ask us to remove your record at any time.
